Stir Fry

Description

Stir Fry is a versatile and quick dish originating from Asian cuisine, beloved for its fresh ingredients and rich flavors. It typically involves cooking a mix of vegetables, proteins, and sauces in a hot pan or wok. The beauty of stir fry is that it can be customized with a variety of ingredients, making it a go-to meal for many households around the world.

Ingredients

These ingredients form the base of a simple stir fry dish. You can add your favorite vegetables, proteins, or seasonings to make it your own!

  • Chicken breast (or beef, shrimp, tofu)
  • Broccoli
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce (optional)
  • Ginger (fresh or ground)
  • Sesame oil (or vegetable oil)
  • Salt and pepper (as preferred)
  • Cooked rice or noodles (for serving)
  • Green onions (optional, for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

Here are the steps I follow to create a delicious and satisfying stir fry. You won't be disappointed!

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Cut your choice of protein (chicken, beef, shrimp, or tofu) into bite-sized pieces.
    • Chop the vegetables: slice bell peppers, carrots, and onions thinly; cut broccoli into florets; and mince garlic and ginger.
  2. Cook the Protein:
    • Heat a tablespoon of sesame oil (or vegetable oil) in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Add the protein to the pan, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned and cooked through (about 4-6 minutes for chicken or beef, less for shrimp). Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the Vegetables:
    • In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil if needed, then toss in the garlic and ginger. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, carrots, and broccoli. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.
  4. Combine the Protein and Vegetables:
    • Return the cooked protein to the pan with the vegetables.
    • Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using) and mix everything together. Let it cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.
    • Taste and adjust seasoning with more soy sauce, salt, or pepper as needed.
  5. Serve the Stir Fry:
    • Serve the stir fry over a bed of steamed rice or noodles for a hearty meal.
    • Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for extra flavor and crunch.

Enjoy your flavorful Stir Fry! It's the perfect quick meal that you can make in under 30 minutes.
